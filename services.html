Health Insurance

We are very proud to be able to bring solutions to business owners that are not available through other Agencies. While we offer the traditional options we have Proprietary and Exclusive solutions that include self-funded and fully insured options with National Employer Health Pools.

One of our Exclusive offerings includes “ON SITE HEALTHCARE CLINICS” for our large Employers, or a group of smaller Employers that want to band together to create the ability to have their own facility for their employees.

Facilities include “Proactive Wellness Physicians” as well as “Private Health Coaches”. Please let us know if you would like more information on this program.

Some of our associations include the following:

  • Compliance Attorney
  • Payroll Companies that are the most qualified in ACA
  • TPA’s to provide full service for self insured or Administration services for HRA/F S A/Wrap Documents/Cobra
