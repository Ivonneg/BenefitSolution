As an employer how do I determine if I need to offer coverage to my employees?

Employers that have ATNE "Average Total Number of Employees" of more than 50 on their wage and tax for the measurement period you have chosen must offer affordable and valuable coverage as defined by the Government.

Do I have to comply with the ACA Compliance of Tracking/Recording & Reporting and 1094 / 1095 filings?

Employers that have over 50 lives on their wage and tax must comply with the Tracking/Reporting and Recording and 1094 and 1095 filings yes.

Can I apply for individual health coverage outside of the “Open Enrollment” period?

YES - Only if you lose coverage due to a life changing event. Only are you eligible to apply for individual coverage outside of National "Open Enrollment" on or off the Exchange.

What are the statistics of me needing Long Term Care?

One in every 2 individuals will need Long Term Care prior to their demise.

Why should I pay for disability insurance?

Protect your income in case of a disability so that you are able to pay for your mortgage/groceries/utilities/other debt/college. 80% of disability cases are denied by social security. Best to protect your income with an individual policy.

How do I plan for Retirement with the time I have left to earn income? Is there a way to Guarantee I will NOT outlive my money?

YES! Guaranteed payouts that will secure an income stream regardless of the account value are great options that are available with bonus upon deposit for immediate accumulation and guaranteed growth.

What is the penalty should I decide I do NOT want to purchase health insurance?

The penalty is 2.5% of your income or $695 for 2016, whichever is greater.
